Code of Conduct During Training

In order that you and your employer derive maximum benefit from the scheme, the following code of conduct have been designed. You are required to adhere to his code of conduct during the entire duration of your training.

Note that the employer's report, ITF form monitors your adherence or other-wise to his code of conduct and constitutes a major part in assessing your participation in SIWES.

  • You should endeavor to be of GOOD BEHAVIOR at all times
  • Be punctual at work
  • Maintain a very good attendance record
  • Maintain a cordial relationship with all members of staff
  • Co-operate with co-workers and your supervisors
  • Show initiative and drive in all your undertakings
  • Take proper care of tools and manuals in your possession
  • Report any difficulty or misunderstanding to your immediate supervisor
  • Be humble so that you can take as much as you are giving to the company
  • Be loyal to the company and keep its secret, you may become an employee of the firm soon
  • Above all, be a good ambassador of your institution
